Resources

Over the years, I've searched high and low for the best resources to help me learn about supply chain and data. From blogs to articles to whitepapers, I've compiled a library of resources. Now I'm sharing a few of my favorites with you.

From demand planning to inventory management to establishing robust business processes, there's a lot out there to learn.

  • Forecast Accuracy and Inventory Strategies, by Demand Planning LLC: for measuring the efficacy of your demand planning processes
  • Crack the Code: Understanding Safety stock and Mastering its Equations, by Peter King (in APICS Magazine): for a deep dive into the analytics behind safety stock planning
  • Oliver Wight Whitepapers, by misc. Oliver Wight consultants: for many whitepapers around demand planning, inventory analytics, data management, and integrated business planning
