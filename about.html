About Me

I get it. As a small business owner, you want to know who you're trusting with your business, so here's the skinny on me.

Origin Story

My undergrad background at Georgia Tech was in operations & supply chain management. I've worked since in various parts of the supply chain, ranging from purchasing to warehousing to demand planning.

Along the way, I realized that I really love data, building tools, and databases. So now I'm working on finishing up a Masters program in Software Development at Boston University.

Not Just a Data Nerd

Yes, I'm a self-admitted data nut. But I'm so much more than that. I'm also a dyed-in-the-wool stereotypical outdoorsy Coloradoan, I'm a passionate hiker, kayaker, and dabble in climbing.

When you reach out about how I can help you, be sure to ask me about my favorite craft brewery, Subaru model, and REI location. I also have to keep my pup supplied with a perpetual supply of chew toys and biscuits.

So don't be shy, now you know me: contact me now!
