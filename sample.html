Sample Results

Let's also assume that your desired service level is 85%. How much safety stock do you need to maintain to ensure you fulfill 85% of all of your customers' orders? Drumroll, please!

Assuming demand and leadtimes stay near current levels, a safety stock of 117 units should be maintained to achieve an 85% service level. What does this mean? On top of the 500 units you expect to sell, you should make sure you have another 117 on hand to account for extreme scenarios.

According to this calculation, with this level of safety stock you should only see a combination of high demand and/or long leadtimes to such a high level that demand exceeds supply in 15% of cases (roughly 55 days of the year).
