Safety Stock Calculator

In this example, I'll show a safety stock calculator for a single product. I'll start with default values for each field, but you can use your own inputs and see how the safety stock changes. This is phrase is in retail terms, but this could also work if you are a manufacturer. Just change the purchase-focused language with production-focused phrasing.

Below is a summary of what each of the inputs means:

  • Average demand: your customers' demand on a weekly basis
  • StDev demand: standard deviation of your customers' demand on a weekly basis
  • Average Leadtime Days: average leadtime from the day you place an order to the day it arrives, in days
  • StDev Leadtime Days: standard deviation of leadtime from the day you place an order to the day it arrives, in days
  • Your Cost/Unit: your cost to buy each unit of inventory
  • Service Level: what percentage of your customers' orders you would like to fulfill

Please note: only positive whole numbers are allowed. Decimals and negatives will be dropped from calculations.
